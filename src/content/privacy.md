This Privacy Policy explains what Realness collects, why, and what you can do
about it. Realness is operated by Scott Fryxell, doing business as Realness
("Realness," "we," "us"), a sole proprietorship in California, USA. It applies
to the hosted service at realness.online (the "Service") and works alongside our
[Terms of Service](/terms).

## The short version

Realness is built to collect as little about you as possible. The original photo
you start from never leaves your device — it is turned into a vector poster
locally, and only what you choose to publish is stored. We do not sell your
data, we do not run advertising, and we do not track you across the web.

## What we collect

- **Your mobile phone number.** Your identity on Realness is derived from your
  phone number. We use it to verify that you are a real person and to let people
  find one another. Verification is done by sending a one-time code.
- **Your profile.** The name you choose and any profile details you add.
- **Content you publish.** The posters, statements, and other content you choose
  to create and share through the Service.
- **Limited technical data.** Basic information needed to operate and secure the
  Service, such as request and error logs handled by our hosting provider.
- **Anti-abuse signals.** When you sign in, Google reCAPTCHA collects device and
  interaction signals to tell humans from bots.

## What we do not collect

- **Your source photos.** The image you start from is processed on your device
  and is not uploaded to or stored by us.
- **Advertising or cross-site tracking.** We don't use advertising cookies or
  sell or share your data with data brokers.

## How we use what we collect

We use the information above to provide and operate the Service, verify and
secure accounts, prevent fraud and abuse, moderate content, respond to you, and
comply with the law. We do not use it for advertising.

## Phone-number-derived identity

Because your identity and the content you create are keyed to an identifier
derived from your phone number, that number is central to how the Service works.
This has two practical effects you should know about:

- **Export** of your content is tied to your verified number.
- **Deletion** of your account removes the data keyed to your identity, but if
  you change phone numbers, content created under the old number may become
  inaccessible to you. Contact us if you need help linking or removing data
  across numbers.

## Service providers (sub-processors)

We rely on a small number of providers to run the Service. They process data on
our behalf under their own terms:

- **Google Firebase** — authentication, file storage, and hosting. Data is
  stored on Google Cloud infrastructure, primarily in the United States.
- **Google reCAPTCHA** — bot and abuse prevention at sign-in.

We may add or change providers as the Service evolves and will update this list
when we do.

## When we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information only when
necessary to: operate the Service through the providers above; comply with a
law, regulation, or valid legal process; enforce our [Terms](/terms); or protect
the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or us.

## Storage on your device

Realness stores data locally on your device — for example in your browser's
local storage and IndexedDB — so the app works well and keeps your work-in-
progress with you. Google reCAPTCHA may set cookies necessary for abuse
prevention. We don't use cookies for advertising.

## Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly
collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under
13 has given us personal information, contact us and we will delete it. Users
under 18 must use the Service with a parent or guardian's involvement.

## Your rights and choices

You can request to access, export, or delete your personal information by
contacting us at **security@realness.online**. We will verify your request using
your phone number, since identity on Realness is phone-derived.

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)
gives you the right to know what personal information we collect, to request
deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and not to be discriminated against
for exercising these rights. We do not sell or "share" personal information as
those terms are defined under California law. To exercise any of these rights,
contact us at the address above.

## Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service and
for legitimate legal, security, and operational purposes. When you delete your
account, we remove the data keyed to your identity, except for limited copies
retained in routine backups for a short period or where the law requires us to
keep them.

## Security

We use reasonable measures, including our providers' security controls, to
protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly
secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you have a security concern,
contact **security@realness.online**.

## International users

The Service is operated from the United States and relies on U.S.-based
infrastructure. If you use it from outside the United States, you understand that
your information will be processed in the United States.
