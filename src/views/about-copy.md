## Artists

**Section title:** Artists

**H2:** We organize **Posters** into thoughts

**Paragraph 1:**  
Take a photo on your phone - or bring a drawing. Realness turns it into a **Poster** with Shadow and Mosaic layers you can export to your tools. A perfect interface between physical and digital drawing.

**H4:** A drawing shaped hole

**Paragraph 2:**  
There is no line work. Though a poster is ornate, it's still missing your hand. The constraints become a vast landscape for you to explore.

**Paragraph 3:**  
Storyboards, collage, thumbnails. No AI in the toolchain. Realness is a clean slate for you to start.

**Paragraph 4:**  
Everything is made locally. Sign in to share across devices.

**H4:**  
Animators, designers, hand-coders - **Realness** was built for you.

**Closing line:**  
One photo. Layered vector art you can export, animate, and share.
